About

Will Wright is an Edinburgh based Front End UI Developer and Digital Designer, with a background in marketing, advertising and graphic design. With over 11 years of experience, he has acquired great knowledge and expertise working alongside award-winning professionals crafting innovative solutions that balance strategy, technology and usability. His core competences include:

Digital & Graphic Design • Front End UI Development in HTML5, CSS3 & SASS, jQuery, AngularJS & JavaScript • Art & Creative Direction • Responsive Web Design • e-Commerce • Google Analytics Certified • Social Media Management • Email Marketing • Display & SEM/PPC (Google Adwords) campaigns • Project & Product Management • Client Relations
